Bacasime Antique is a DocRepair font, intended to improve compliance with the ISO/IEC 29500 standard by providing fallback for Baskerville Old Face that minimizes text reflow in Office Open XML documents. Bacasime Antique is based on Playfair, which is stylistically a transitional design.

To contribute, please visit github.com/docrepair-fonts/bacasime-antique-fonts.
